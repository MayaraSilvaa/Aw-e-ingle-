The Games Market in the Pandemic

In the pandemic, the market for buying and selling games increases by more than 100%. A study carried out by the Visa card brand concluded that, in 2020, there was a 140% increase in financial transactions made on the main gaming platforms and consoles compared to 2019.

Never played so much in Brazil. The advantages of the home office, such as the flexibility of working hours, the well-being generated by working in a family environment and greater use of working time, such as the loss of commuting in traffic, allowed Brazilians to be more able to spend the time having fun. And, for many, games are the best option. In addition, the increase in the consumption of live streaming games also increased, as shown by the e-commerce site Brazil, by 20% in April 2020, generating more and more interest and discussion on the subject at hand and more consumption. This ends up causing, consequently, more purchases.
It is a typical trend that gradually develops due to the change in routine and transforming the market. New sites, such as Desapego Games, are emerging with the intention of conquering those customers who are more willing to invest. Video games have already become routine in our country. About 75.8% of Brazilian gamers said they played more during this period, according to the Gamers Brazil Survey (PGB).


Relationship of games with health, and immersion in the gamer universe.

Lately people have been looking for games as a way to get out of reality and immerse themselves in a totally different world and with many new things to be discovered, this is one of the reasons that most captivate players and buy or become players of a match. In times to come, such as the corona virus pandemic, many people started to stay much longer at home and change that famous ballad, for a while in a game, having fun and communicating by "discord", for example, which is one of the means of communication widely used by players, for reasons such as: better interaction with friends, free contact for as many hours as possible, and also the ability to gather several in one call.
Through these means, young people prefer to maintain communication and prefer to stay at home, in the midst of all circumstances, we concluded that for today it is something beneficial, as young people and adults today can choose this method to talk and play at the same time. Lately it has been very effective, this as a method of staying isolated, but not far from your friends/relatives.

Final considerations

It is noteworthy that games are not something that emerged from yesterday to today, but these past few times have been very important for depression prevention, so that in moments of isolation people could maintain communication, do something that they have fun, have what do on a day-to-day basis and not just complain about not being able to leave the house. It is clear, therefore, that games have become the foundation for many, the market has been growing, and the desire to have access to computers, consoles and everything else that enables a good game is growing.
